Why Huddle cafe is different from others?

Why Huddle cafe is different from others?

Huddles cafe is a family-owned restaurant in Trivandrum, Kerala, with a proud history of discovering and blending dishes to gratify everyone palates. We specialize in authentic Greek, Italian, American, and Indian fare, made with the finest, organic, and home-grown ingredients – many produced by the local farmers in Kerala. We love making customers feel that they’re eating at a homely cafe, so we could keep our standards at their heart. We are one of the best burger joints restaurants in Trivandrum (see our reviews). And we offer great deals on burgers, sandwiches, pizzas, and the rest of dishes in our signature menu (over 100 dishes) with a cozy lounge to sit and savor. We also offer a variety of healthy and delicious dishes, and our friendly staff will make you feel like a family. We have also been recommended as the best place for techies, besties, and lovers. We welcome everyone to our Huddles Cafe if you’re visiting Trivandrum. For those are already living in our town, don’t hesitate as you don’t want to miss the best café in Trivandrum with an ambiance soothing as it can get.

SURF & TURF

This is one of our main courses, Surf & Turf marries beef lender loin with prawn sauce. A classic dish, it is often featured as a main course at many Australian family meet-ups. Roasted potatoes or mushrooms make a good combination with this dish. It’s a dish the whole family would enjoy. Prawns are also an excellent source of unsaturated fat which make up for most of their fat content. On the healthy side, rarely do we get a source of protein which is both low fat and low calorie. Shrimps are packed with proteins and other minerals such as vitamins A and E, phosphorous, potassium, and calcium. This makes them good for the growth of tissue and bone and supports the structure of enamel in place. They don’t place stress on blood vessels and improve cholesterol levels, unlike saturated fats which risk clotting blood.

TOWER BURGER

Then, the fries asked, “Can we be on board too?” As years went by, the chefs experimented and created some of the wackiest combinations to satiate their quest for new dishes. Today, there are classic takes with a contemporary hint, and there are some completely nouveau burgers that have made the cut.
